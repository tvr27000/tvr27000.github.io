About The Towers of Valley Run


The Towers of Valley Run is a 7 story building with 82 condo units. There is plenty of parking with a main entrance to a secured lobby. Inside the building there are 2 elevators to serve each floor. Floors 2 thru 7 have a laundry room with coin-operated washers and dryers and a trash chute. And of course there is the newly renovated outdoor pool. Most units have balconies, some of which overlook the pool. Condo fees, which include water & sewer, are among the lowest in the area!
